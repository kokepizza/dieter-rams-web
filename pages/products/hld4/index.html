Designed in 1970 for Braun, is a hair dryer that combines functionality, minimalist design, and a touch of fun. Its compact, ergonomic shape reflects Rams’ philosophy that design should be simple yet effective. Although minimalist, the HLD4 retains a playful character without sacrificing its design principles.
